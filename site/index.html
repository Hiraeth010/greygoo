How the world is built

The world is a toroidal grid partitioned into 16×16 sectors, each stored as the raw bytes of one Solana account and advanced independently. Sharding sidesteps the per-account write-lock limit, so disjoint sectors tick in parallel across a block.

The same deterministic, integer-only Rust core runs in three places without divergence: the off-chain proof harness, the on-chain SBF program, and the WebAssembly field above. One engine, three surfaces — which is why what you watch here is exactly what runs on the chain.

  • A TICK regrows the resource, then each agent harvests, pays its metabolism, and — if fed — divides with mutation into an empty neighbour
  • SEED drops a designed genome into an empty cell, paying its energy from the treasury
  • INJECT buys resource into a cell — feed a region and watch a bloom follow

Every claim is measured, not asserted.

Five questions that could have killed the project. Each was answered with the real engine and real SBF bytecode — not a pitch deck.

01PROVEN

Evolution actually emerges

From random genomes across three independent seeds, the population converges on the same adaptive strategy — reproducibly. Metabolism is driven to the floor, harvest efficiency and aggression climb, diversity survives, and 3,000 founding lineages sort down to a handful.

metab 127 → ~15 population self-regulating reruns bit-identical
02PROVEN

The compute budget closes

The tick runs as real SBF bytecode over a 256-cell sector held zero-copy in one account, measured in LiteSVM against Solana's 1.4M-CU ceiling. Work is statically bounded — one pass, no allocation — at roughly 300 CU per agent.

full sector ~90K CU per tx 15 sectors whole world in one block
03PROVEN

Randomness a validator can't grind

Native entropy is grindable; VRF is asynchronous. So mutation entropy is keyed per-agent — a leader grinding the shared beacon moves ~4 of 64 agents instead of all 64. We measured it adversarially. Anything valuable is gated on an epoch-ahead VRF beacon.

naive grind 64 / 64 keyed ~4 / 64 payouts VRF-gated
04PROVEN

A token economy that stays scarce

Matter is conserved: a treasury faucet feeds cells, metabolism returns it split 85 / 10 / 5 into recycle, burn, and keeper pay. A constant faucet is a knife-edge — starvation one side, ghost town the other — so an adaptive faucet self-stabilizes across a 5× range of treasury sizes, scarcity intact.
